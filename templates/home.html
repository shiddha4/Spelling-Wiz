    The new way of learning spelling

    Most kids learn spelling the old way, however spelling wiz is introducing the new way of learning how to spell. No, not big text books because the reality students can never finish the books and even when they finish they forget everything the next day. Using Artificial intelligence spelling wiz takes your child to the next level. For example, if a kid can do the easy word our website takes him through the more difficult and complex words. We have spelling words by grade level. From pre-k to high school words.

    Spelling helps with reading

    Learning to spell helps to pronounce words which directly helps with reading.


    Spelling is extremely useful for life

    From report papers to resumes, you need to learn to spell. If you don't have good spelling then your teacher or professor may penalize for the bad spelling.
